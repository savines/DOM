Dance of Minds: AI - Human Interaction consists of 20 conversations between me (Haluk Aykul y.) and ChatGPT and Claude, and it is an experimental co-generation journey to travel to undiscovered territories of “AI-Human Interaction”, to execute a beautiful "Dance of Minds".

The whole book is based on a "generovocative approach" (multi-dimensional recursive loops of thought-provoking generations and thought experiments).

Each section can be read separately. Some of them are based on epistemological discussions about consciousness, emotions, intelligence etc., and some of them are more creative conversations with hypothetical deep thinking.
